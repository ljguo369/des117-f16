+Biography+

Chaelin Lee also known by her stage name CL, is a South Korean singer-songwriter; the rapper and leader of a girl group called 2NE1. CL is born in Seoul, South Korea on February 26, 1991, but she spent most of her childhood living in Paris, Tsukuba Science City, and Tokyo. When she was 15 year old, she got addition into the YG entertainment and debut in 2009.

The group name “2NE1” includes the meanings “To anyone,“ “21 century,“ and “forever young like 21.“ On May 6, 2009, the YG entertainment release two music video versions of their debut song “Fire”which igniting the fire throughout the Kpop world. The popularity of 2NE1 continues to grow with their follow up albums and digital singles.

CL's first solo single, “The Baddest Female,“ was released on May 28,2013. The song is a mixture of elements from Hip Hop, Dougie, and Dubstep which prominent CL's characteristics. In 2014, CL team up with Scooter Braunn and debut as a solo artist in the United States with hit songs “Hello Bitches“ and “Lifted,“
